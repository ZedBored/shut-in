Shut In

A story of a teenager who prefers to be shut in, not interacting outside his house. He is surving on his own by the means of earning as a freelancer and depending fully on internet.



Instructions


  • Here, 1 second of our world = 2 min in their world
  • Survive by sleeping, eating and earning money.
  • Remember to eat and sleep to fill your stats, if your hunger or sleep bar goes down it may cause serious issues or even END THE GAME
  • Earn money by either Freelancing or completing surveys, you can also push your luck for a lottery.
